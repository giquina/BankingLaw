What are the current PRA capital requirements for UK banks?
JuriBank

UK banks must maintain minimum capital ratios under Basel III as implemented by the PRA:

  • CET1 ratio: 4.5% + capital conservation buffer (2.5%) = 7%
  • Tier 1 ratio: 6% + buffer = 8.5%
  • Total capital ratio: 8% + buffer = 10.5%

Additional buffers may apply for systemically important institutions.
