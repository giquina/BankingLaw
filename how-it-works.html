Common Questions

Everything you need to know about using JuriBank

Is JuriBank really free to start?

Yes! Our eligibility checker, basic calculators, knowledge base, and community forum are completely free. You only pay if you want premium features like advanced tools, personal dashboard, and priority student support.

Are law students qualified to help?

Our law students are studying LLB degrees at top London universities and are supervised by qualified solicitors. They provide educational guidance to help you understand your options - not legal advice. When you need professional representation, we connect you with qualified solicitors.

How do you match me with the right solicitor?

Our matching quiz considers your case type, location, budget, and preferences. We only refer SRA-regulated solicitors with proven experience in your specific area. You get 2-3 carefully selected matches, not a generic directory.

What if my case isn't successful?

We help you understand your realistic chances of success upfront. Many solicitors in our network offer "No Win, No Fee" arrangements. We'll also help you understand all your options, including alternative dispute resolution.

How long does the process take?

It varies by case type: bank charge claims typically take 2-8 weeks, PPI claims can take 2-6 months, and complex investment disputes may take 6-18 months. We provide realistic timelines and keep you updated throughout.
