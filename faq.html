Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about using JuriBank

Is JuriBank really free to start?

Yes! Our eligibility checker, basic calculators, knowledge base, and community forum are completely free. You only pay if you want premium features like advanced tools, personal dashboard, and priority student support (Â£9.99/month).

Are law students qualified to help?

Our law students are studying LLB degrees at top London universities and are supervised by qualified solicitors. They provide educational guidance to help you understand your options - not legal advice. When you need professional representation, we connect you with qualified solicitors.

How do you match me with the right solicitor?

Our matching quiz considers your case type, location, budget, and preferences. We only refer SRA-regulated solicitors with proven experience in your specific area. You get 2-3 carefully selected matches, not a generic directory.

What if my case isn't successful?

We help you understand your realistic chances of success upfront. Many solicitors in our network offer "No Win, No Fee" arrangements. We'll also help you understand all your options, including alternative dispute resolution.

How long does the process take?

It varies by case type: bank charge claims typically take 2-8 weeks, PPI claims can take 2-6 months, and complex investment disputes may take 6-18 months. We provide realistic timelines and keep you updated throughout.

Do I need to live in London to use JuriBank?

No! While our law students study at London universities, we help people across the UK. Our tools work for all UK banks and financial institutions, and we have solicitors throughout England, Wales, Scotland, and Northern Ireland.

What's the difference between student guidance and legal advice?

Student guidance helps you understand legal concepts, processes, and your options. Legal advice involves specific recommendations for your situation and can only be given by qualified solicitors. We're clear about this boundary and help you access both as appropriate.

Can I use JuriBank if I already have a solicitor?

Absolutely! Our knowledge base, calculators, and community can complement your existing legal representation. Many users find our resources helpful for understanding their case better and preparing questions for their solicitor.

How do you keep information up to date?

We integrate directly with Gov.UK, FCA, Financial Ombudsman Service, and other official APIs to pull real-time information. Our law students also monitor regulatory changes and court decisions to keep our guidance current.

What happens to my personal information?

We're fully GDPR compliant and only collect information necessary to help you. Personal details are encrypted, never sold to third parties, and you can delete your account anytime. When we refer you to solicitors, they handle your data under their own privacy policies.
